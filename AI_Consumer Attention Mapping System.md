## Consumer Attention Mapping System 

## 1. Title: Consumer Attention Mapping System 

## 2. Objective 

- Build an AI powered Consumer Attention Mapping System that uses cameras, computer vision, behavioral analytics, and AI models to understand how shoppers interact with retail shelves, products, promotional displays, and store layouts. 

The platform analyzes customer attention patterns, gaze direction, dwell time, product engagement, movement paths, and purchase interactions to help retailers optimize shelf placement, improve product visibility, increase conversion rates, - and enhance in store customer experiences. 

The solution is designed for retail stores, supermarkets, shopping malls, FMCG companies, consumer brands, retail analytics teams, and marketing organizations.

- Implemented consumer behavior modeling and attention heatmaps. 

- Built conversion analysis and product attractiveness scoring engines. 

- Developed dashboards for retail intelligence and merchandising analytics. 

- Deployed the platform using Docker and cloud deployment platforms such as AWS or Azure.
